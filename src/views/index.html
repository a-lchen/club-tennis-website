LATEST NEWS

Qualified for 2019 Nationals!
10/20/2019

Back2Back. We’re going back to Nationals for the second time in MIT club tennis history, after achieving the feat for the first time last season. #tennisoncampus After winning our pool on Saturday, we lost a tight match Sunday morning against Harvard by a single game, coming back during overtime, but ultimately losing a deciding super tiebreaker. After matches against NE and BU, we clinched the fifth and final spot to Nationals from the New England region (among 31 teams). Let’s go MIT!
Qualified for 2018 Nationals!
9/28/2019

For the first time in MIT club tennis history, we qualified for the USTA Tennis on Campus National tournament. After three days of intense competition and quality tennis against the top clubs in the country, we earned a spot among the top 25 teams in the nation. We look forward to going back this year!
